The Portfolio of Just...

An Account of the Study of Game Design & Audio Engineering at Yale

Herein lies a chronicle of my scholarly pursuit at Yale University, a journey into the bygone eras of interactive media. My focus is the careful crafting of digital antiquities, where each pixel and synthesized note is a deliberate choice. Through the art of 8-bit retro creation, I seek to design new worlds that evoke the nostalgia and magic of a simpler time. This folio serves as a modest testament to that grand undertaking.
